FAQs

What is this?

Bring your plant clippings and extra seedlings, leave with new plants from others. Plus drinks, an open grill, light music, glow lights, and community.

When and where?

Saturday, March 21st, 5 – 10 PM in Inglewood, East Nashville, Tennessee. I'll send you the address after you RSVP. Late arrivals welcome – just check in with me after 10.

There's a quiet space available if you need a breather. The venue has steps – let me know if you have access needs.

Who is this for?

Everyone who likes plants! No experience or supplies required. Want to bring a friend? Give me a heads up and note it on the RSVP.

What should I bring?

Seedlings and cuttings to share – please label them with the plant name and basic care tips. You're also welcome to bring full grown plants, seed banks, sharable foods (the grill will be on!), drinks, or whatever else – just be ready to share! If you bring food, label ingredients for folks with dietary restrictions.

I'll have temporary containers and soil on hand. Extra pots are always welcome too.

What should I not bring?

Pests. Wash your plants.

Parent plants. Don't bring a plant that you don't intend to share all of.

Pets. Berry, Melon, Blu, and Regina will be holding court and we'd like to protect the plants.

Why so long?

Folks can drop by anytime during this. Starting earlier gives us some daylight and supports folks that have evening plans. This will go into the night though, it is in fact a party!

Any ground rules?

No selling – this is a market based on abundance, kindness, and multiplicative growth. Some attendees have plant-related businesses, so take the chance to learn about and support each other.

Be kind. Ask before photographing people you don't know. Use the names, pronouns, and words people ask you to use. If you feel unsafe, reach out to me ASAP.

Health & allergies?

Please stay home if you're feeling sick. I have two cats, Melon and Blu – allergens included, unfortunately.

Other questions?

Drop a note on your RSVP or ask me directly! I'll add to this page if it helps others.
